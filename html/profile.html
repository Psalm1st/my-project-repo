Introduction

My name is Samuel Chukwu; also known as Amicable. I am Aquarius by Zodiac; born on the 30th of January in the South-eastern part of Nigeria. I am actually the last fruit of my parents. I recently moved to the Netherlands to further my education and I live in Middelburg, Zeeland. The idea had always been to study somewhere outside my comfort zone, which poised my choice to study here at HZ University of Applied Science as an ideal destination to build my lifelong career.

Hobbies

I really love to play soccer. I am an avid football fan and supporter of Manchester United FC. When I was little, due to the great love I had for playing, I joined a community club, where I played as left back. I grew up with the same passion for the game. As a matter of fact, I was awarded the Best football Player back in Secondary School, for 2015/2016 Academic Year, after I led my RedHouse team to victory, during our annual inter-house Sport game.

I am also a great fan of music. Often times, I listen to music when I have pile of works to do around the house. It actually makes work less tedious. Having music around, I can work like a robot. They love to have me around; where we would play all day, as well as watch cartoons together. They made me love anime and some other cartoons too.

I really love nature and outdoor activities as well, like sight-seeing and going to the beach. I also like dangerous stuff that will give me a trill; for example, I want to go parachute diving or bungy jumping someday.
