My motivation for ICT

My innate interest while growing up has been to broaden my knowledge on the use of computers and its application to facilitate scientific research in solving professional and social issues. I have been influenced by the ICT course and the channel of specialization it offers, which focuses on the most intriguing aspect of Programming, Software designs, Business intelligence, Web development and so on.

I have always given thought to how each and everyone would benefit me in the future, hence, why I would love to take my education to a higher level, with an opportunity to enhance my current understanding through studying at one of the prestigious destinations. With utmost confidence, I believe that spending most of my time studying my dream IT course here, would not only enable me to receive an exceptional training in the field, but allow me gain new perspectives into professionalism and be part of the vital instruments for my nation.

Why I find ICT suitable

The ICT programme fits me because, in the ICT field you get a lot of freedom when working on projects. My future career goal has always been to become a Software Developer with great knowledge on IT field, generally; and be part of the essential marshals that leverage modern tools, as well as adopt best practices to lead businesses in complex and uncertain environments. I would also love to broaden my horizon to acquire more knowledge and expertise in terms of breadth and depth, in order to avail myself the opportunity to work around experts, which will further open door for me to develop a salient skill required to optimize the effectiveness of ICT in a wider range of application that cuts across Robotics, Data Analytics and Cyber-information security.

I have always envisaged to be part of the essential instrument that will help bridge the digital divide; help those who often lag behind, assist local businesses scale up to a global level and create more employable computer experts that will meet the standards of the modern world. Moreover, I will continue to apply the same diligence I must have acquired, into making service to others my top priority and contribute to the basic aim of making the world a global village.
