FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q:How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A: First go to print.hz.nl. Sign in with your HZ username and password. When you're logged in, upload the file you want to print. Select any of the printers (located on every corridor) you would want to use and confirm it. Afterwards, go to the printer you selected, scan your HZ card therein and select print. Then follow the instructions on the printer and you are done!.

Q: How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

A: If you go to any of the printers, scan your HZ card, and you can then choose to scan your document on the same machine. When done, the document will be sent to your school email automatically.

Q: How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

A: You will need to enter HZ webshop.hz.nl page. On the menu, select the "Log in" option and do so. Afterwards, you can be able to source tickets; further, order items or enrol in for some other events/activities.

Q: Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

A: For all the necessary information about COVID and related instructions, you can visit hz-covid-update. In the alternative, you can click to call the school topdesk or message any of your teachers via Teams asap, informing them of your sickness or signs of COVID; they would be in the best position to advice you on the safest measures to take.

Q: How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A: Go to ss.portal and log in. Then follow this simple steps > New loction reservation > Reserve meeting room or Project group room > Reserve a room and you will see the schedule of the rooms you can choose from and there you can reserve one for a specific time frame.

Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

A: You will need to reveal your HZ card to the parking barrier gates, which will then recognise your identity and thereby grants you access to the parking section for both students and HZ employees, so you would park at the space rounded in barbed wires.
